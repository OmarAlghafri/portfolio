Overview

What is a Convolutional Neural Network?

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is a specialized type of artificial neural network specifically developed for processing grid-like data such as images.

This technology is inspired by how the human brain processes visual information, using multiple layers of artificial neurons to extract features and patterns from images.

In the medical field, these networks are used to analyze medical images like X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans, helping doctors with early and accurate disease diagnosis.
